About me

I am a second year PhD student studying Cognitive Neuroscience at the Universtity of California, Irvine. I am interested in learning how the brain efficiently encodes stimuli in the world. Specifically, I focus on how motion and spatial information is represented and retrieved during navigational behaviors. I am a member of the Cognitive Anteater Robotics Laboratory and am advised by Prof. Jeff Krichmar.
